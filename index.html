About Me

 My name is Alexis Peves. I have a degree in Mechanical Engineering and spent most of my professional experience as a manufacturing engineer. I am currently training to pursue a career as a software engineer.

 I have always been interested in learning different trades and skills that I can use throughout life. Started with learning construction trades(plumbing, electrical, carpentry, and HVAC) in vo-tech high school. After high school I explored automotive work(both body and mechanical work) with friends and family. I like to challenge myself to learn different things.

 After college, I took up some hobbies/crafts to take up some free time. One in particular stuck with me over the years and I keep coming back to it over the years. Crochet has been a favorite of mine because it's relaxing and you can make some beautiful projects. This crocodile and this llama are my two most detailed projects I've made so far.
